My life

I was born in New Zealand, on Waiheke Island (An island off the coast of Auckland) on the 19th of September, 2009. I was a cheeky little bugger as a kid, and annoyed a lot of my neighbours. Some might still consider me a cheeky little bugger but I'm much older now, and much less frantic and cheeky. Growing up, I played a lot of rugby, but I never enjoyed it that much. I started playing basketball in year 8 and I still do to this day, so that usually keeps my health up. Gym is also a big thing for me, as I go quite often and I have also started a bit of martial arts. I found passion in web development in 2023 during my half year Digital Media class, and I have never stopped enjoying website development.

I wish to continue my skills in website development by taking digital media next year with my course selection, alongside computer science to implement more script into my websites. After school, I'm planning to study digital technologies in college, and then work for a company in website development. I may also choose to start my own business with web design and design websites for various companies. Over the next few years, I am hoping to increase my skills with web design, creating more and more complex and better looking websites. I find creativity one of the greatest human features, as it powers most of the amazing designs in the world. Statues, art and everything in between.
